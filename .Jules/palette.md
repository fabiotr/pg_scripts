## 2025-03-15 - Professionalism and Consistency in SQL Script Naming
**Learning:** Typos in filenames and reporting headers (e.g., "labes", "reciever") and unprofessional language in scripts (e.g., "fucking") degrade user trust and the perceived quality of the tool. Inconsistent casing for acronyms like "WAL" or "SSL" in reports makes them harder to scan and less visually cohesive.
**Action:** Proactively audit filenames and output headers for typos and professionalism. Standardize the casing of technical acronyms and ensure descriptive labels are pluralized correctly in summary reports.
