## 2025-05-14 - [Corrected Recurring Typo in Report UI]
**Learning:** In projects where "UI" is a generated Markdown report or terminal output, spelling consistency in headers and filenames is a critical micro-UX component that affects professionalism and trust.
**Action:** Always check for recurring typo patterns in report headers and internal meta-commands (`\qecho`, `\i`) when working with script-based reporting tools.
