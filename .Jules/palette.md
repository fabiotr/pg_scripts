## 2025-05-15 - [Standardize report outputs to English]
**Learning:** For a CLI-based tool suite (like SQL scripts), the "user interface" is the output of the commands. Inconsistency in language (e.g., mixing Portuguese and English) can make the tool feel less professional and less accessible to a global audience.
**Action:** Always check for hardcoded language strings in output aliases and formatting logic (like `sed` scripts) to ensure a consistent, accessible experience for all users.
